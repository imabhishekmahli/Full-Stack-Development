Biography

Linus Torvalds is a finnish-american software engineer who is the creator and principle developer of the linux kernel.which is the kernel for a number of operating system. he also created the vesion control system git.torvalds was born on december 28,1969. in helsinki,finland.he studied computer science at the univesity of helsinki.and he developed the linux kernel as a student project in 1991. since then the linux kernel has become one of the most widely user kernel in the world.powering everything form smartphones to supercomputers.
